## Introduction 
Hello! I'm Maanasa Gogula, a Computer Engineering student at Purdue University with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

My passion for Computer Engineering started as I grew up in a household of software engineers and all that technical jargon was regular dinner talk. Being surrounded by that environment made me naturally curious about how software systems worked and how technology could be used to solve real-world problems.

Starting college, I realized that what genuinely excited me most was building and working with technology. I found myself drawn not only to writing code, but also to understanding how systems are designed, optimized, and deployed at scale. Currently, I’m particularly interested in agentic AI, AI engineering, full-stack development, and distributed systems. I enjoy working on projects that combine intelligent automation with practical software engineering, and I’m especially excited by the growing potential of large language models and AI-powered applications.
